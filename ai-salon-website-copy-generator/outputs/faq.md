Q1: Do I need to book an appointment or can I walk in?

Both are welcome! That said, we recommend booking in advance — especially on weekends or before festivals and weddings.

We recommend booking at least 3–4 weeks before your wedding date.

Q3: Are the products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. We use professional-grade, salon-approved products and perform checks before treatments.

Q4: What's the difference between a regular facial and your treatments?

Our treatments target specific concerns like pigmentation, acne, dullness, or dehydration.

Q5: What is keratin treatment and how long does it last?

Keratin smoothens hair, reduces frizz, and typically lasts 3–5 months.
